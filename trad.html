Mural Paintings

Origin

The mural art is believed to have originated during the palaeolithic age; these paintings were painted on the stones in caves, tombs, or ceilings. In India, many mural paintings were dated to the 14th century and were found in Kerala.

Tanjore Paintings

Origin

Originating in the city of Thanjavur in Tamil Nadu, India, these paintings were created during the grand era of the Chola dynasty and the Nayak period, and reached the zenith of their cultural significance during the Maratha period in the 18th century.
